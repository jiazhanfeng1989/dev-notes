### Tips
```
Press + hold right mouse button (anywhere on screen):
↓→: Close current tab
↓→↑: Open new window
↑←：Move to left tab
↑→：Move to right tab
↑↓: Refresh
↑↓↑: Force refresh
←: Go back
→: Go forward
→↓: Scroll to bottom
→↑: Scroll to top

Press + hold left mouse button and drag link to:
→: Open link in new tab
```
